About Dhorleey Tacos

Dhorleey Tacos was founded in 2011. Our shop was built from the love of Tacos. We hope that our "Place of Tacos" adds a unique and interesting piece to this town of Ilaro!


Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in Nigeria?

Tunmise N. Egunleti, taco historian and professor of history at the University of Ilorin, says the earliest mention of tacos in Nigeria are in a newspaper from 1995.
(Source: Punch Newspaper)
